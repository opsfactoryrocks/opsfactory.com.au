Gotchas and limitations

  • AZ naming differs from one customer account to the next. Your us-west-1a is not the same as another customers us-west-1a the letters are assigned to physical AZs randomly per account. This can also be a gotcha if you have multiple AWS accounts.
  • Cross-AZ traffic is not free. At large scale, the costs add up to a significant amount of money. If possible, optimize your traffic to stay within the same AZ as much as possible.
